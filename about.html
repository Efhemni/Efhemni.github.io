Welcome to Efhemni!

Efhemni is a platform for university students to share concerns, connect with others, and access mental health resources in a safe, supportive environment.

Our Mission

We aim to reduce the stigma around mental health by fostering a compassionate community where students feel safe and empowered.

Why Choose Efhemni?

University life can be challenging. Efhemni provides emotional support, connections, and professional help to navigate these challenges.

Our Services

  • Anonymous Posting: Share your thoughts and struggles without revealing your identity.
  • Account Creation: Connect with others and track your mental health journey.
  • Online Psychiatrist Sessions: Book one-on-one sessions with licensed professionals for 30DT/hour.
  • Mental Health Resources: Access articles, tips, and tools to manage your mental health.

Book a Session
