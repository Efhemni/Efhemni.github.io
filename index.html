Welcome to Efhemni!

Your go-to platform for mental well-being. We offer a safe space for university students to anonymously share their thoughts, concerns, and experiences related to their mental health. You can also book one-on-one sessions with licensed psychiatrists to get professional help.

Our mission is to provide a supportive and understanding community for students navigating the challenges of university life. Whether you're struggling with stress, anxiety, or just need someone to talk to, Efhemni is here to help.

Book a Session with a Psychiatrist

If you're looking for professional help, Efhemni allows you to book a session with a licensed psychiatrist. The cost is 30DT per hour. Sessions are available online, making it easier than ever to seek help from the comfort of your home. Book your session now!
